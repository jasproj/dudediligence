Partner FAQs

When do I get paid?

Commissions are paid on the 1st of each month via PayPal, Stripe transfer, or cryptocurrency. Minimum payout is $50. If you don't hit the minimum, it rolls over to the next month.

How long do referrals last?

Forever. We use lifetime cookie tracking (365-day cookie + account attribution). If someone clicks your link and signs up 6 months later, you still get credit.

Can I promote via paid ads?

Yes, but no bidding on "Dude Diligence" branded keywords (Google Ads, etc.). You can run ads targeting "due diligence tools," "commodity trading risk," etc. Check partner agreement for full policy.

What if my referral cancels?

You stop earning commission when they cancel. But if they reactivate later, commission resumes automatically. No claw-backs on previous months you were already paid.

Do you offer higher commissions for volume?

Yes. Partners who refer 20+ paying customers get bumped to 35%. Partners who refer 50+ get 40%. Enterprise white-label deals are negotiated individually.
