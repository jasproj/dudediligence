How Dude Diligence Works

A multi-layered intelligence framework for commodity trade verification

Intelligence-Grade Due Diligence

Dude Diligence employs a proprietary multi-vector analysis system originally developed for financial crime investigation. Our platform synthesizes data from multiple independent sources, cross-references against global watchlists, and applies behavioral pattern recognition to identify fraud indicators invisible to traditional screening methods.

Unlike basic document checkers, our system examines relationships between data points to detect sophisticated fraud schemes including identity recycling, document forgery networks, and coordinated scam operations.

Five-Layer Verification Framework

1

Document Intelligence Extraction

Advanced natural language processing extracts structured data from unstructured documents (LOIs, ICPOs, KYC packages). Our AI identifies not just what is written, but how it's written—detecting template reuse, copy-paste patterns, and linguistic inconsistencies that indicate fraud.

Entity extraction Pattern analysis Linguistic forensics
2

Identity & Entity Verification

Every entity is cross-referenced against international registries, corporate databases, and trade association records. We verify company registration, beneficial ownership structures, and director networks—revealing shell companies, dissolved entities, and fraudulent business identities.

Corporate registry lookup Beneficial ownership Director networks
3

Global Sanctions & Watchlist Screening

Real-time screening against OFAC, UN, EU, and Interpol databases. Our system checks not just exact name matches, but phonetic variants, aliases, and known associates—catching sanctioned parties attempting to operate under alternate identities.

OFAC SDN screening UN consolidated list Alias detection
4

Behavioral Pattern Recognition

Proprietary algorithms analyze trading behavior, volume claims, payment structures, and communication patterns. We identify red flags invisible to human reviewers: unrealistic quantities, suspicious payment terms (100% upfront), free email domains, and historical fraud indicators.

Volume anomaly detection Payment pattern analysis Fraud signature matching
5

Document Fingerprinting & Network Analysis

Cryptographic hashing creates unique document fingerprints. We track document reuse across multiple deals—exposing serial scammers who recycle the same LOI with different buyer names. Our network graph analysis reveals connections between seemingly unrelated fraud operations.

SHA-256 fingerprinting Reuse tracking Network graph analysis

Proprietary Risk Scoring Algorithm

Each verification layer generates threat indicators weighted by severity and confidence. Our scoring engine synthesizes these signals into a 0-100 risk score with color-coded classifications:

GREEN
85-100 • Low risk • Proceed with standard diligence
YELLOW
60-84 • Moderate risk • Request additional documentation
RED
30-59 • High risk • Enhanced due diligence required
BLACK
0-29 • Critical risk • Do not proceed

The algorithm continuously learns from new fraud patterns, ensuring detection accuracy improves over time without manual rule updates.

Our Limitations (Transparency)

Dude Diligence is a screening and analysis tool, not a replacement for comprehensive legal due diligence. We provide intelligence to inform your decisions, but cannot:

  • • Guarantee 100% fraud detection (sophisticated operations may evade automated systems)
  • • Provide legal advice or regulatory compliance certification
  • • Access non-public law enforcement or classified intelligence databases
  • • Verify physical commodities, warehouse receipts, or on-site operations

Always conduct independent verification and consult legal counsel before finalizing high-value transactions.
